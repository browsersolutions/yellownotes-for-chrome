Yellow Sticky-notes for the web

Ever created a browser bookmark and forgotten why ? Yellow Sticky-notes where a brilliant invention when they arrive decades ago. Scribble something on it and stick it anywhere. As a reminder to your-self or others. As a bookmark with notes on the text where you can't write directly in the document itself. Change your mind ? No need to erase, just throw the note away. Which you can't do if you write on the page itself. Yes, the little yellow notes where perfect, and justifiably enormously successfull. This is sticky-notes for the web. Stick them straight on a web page and scribble you notes on then. When next you are back to the same place, the note is right there waiting for you. You highlight a piece of text and the note is attached to this highlighted area. Where bookmarks, Or "like" and "share" in social media, are crude whole-article/page/document selectiing tools - the Yellow Sticky-Note for Web you select only the piece you are interested in. And add your remarks are tied to only this.
